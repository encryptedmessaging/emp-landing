Communication Secured.

Encrypted Messaging Protocol

EMP uses industry leading encryption and peer-to-peer delivery to improve privacy and stability.

Open Source & P2P

EMP is free and open. The local client allows users to run EMP on their computers for free.

Industry Leading Encryption

EMP uses the same technology as Bitcoin and BitMessage to protect your communications.

Built for Performance

The EMP Local client is written in go. It's designed to work on even low powered systems.

Modular

Designed with extensibility in mind. EMP can be used by any application to securely move data.
